Selected Publications

  1. Learning Convex Decomposition via Feature Fields.
    Yuezhi Yang ,  Qixing Huang ,  Mikaela Angelina Uy ,  Nicholas Sharp
    Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) , 2026
    TLDR PDF Video Website

    A feed-forward model for convex decomposition of 3D shapes by learning a continuous feature field where distances between point embeddings indicate whether they belong to the same convex component, trained by a geometric contrastive loss.

  2. ART-DECO: Arbitrary Text Guidance for 3D Detailizer Construction.
    Qimin Chen ,  Yuezhi Yang ,  Yifan Wang ,  Vladimir G. Kim ,  Siddhartha Chaudhuri ,  Hao Zhang ,  Zhiqin Chen
    SIGGRAPH Asia(Conference) , 2025
    TLDR PDF Website

    A reusable neural 3D detailizer that converts a coarse shape proxy into a detailed 3D asset within seconds, by distilling knowledge from a pretrained multi-view diffusion model via score distillation sampling.

  3. An Efficient Global-to-Local Rotation Optimization Approach via Spherical Harmonics.
    Zihang He ,  Yuezhi Yang ,  Congyue Deng ,  Jiaxin Lu ,  Leonidas Guibas ,  Qixing Huang
    Symposium on Geometry Processing (SGP) , 2025
    TLDR PDF

    A global-to-local optimization method for estimating the relative rotation between two 3D shapes, by aligning spherical harmonic coefficients of spherical distance functions with a robust weighted objective.

  4. GenAnalysis: Joint Shape Analysis by Learning Man-Made Shape Generators with Deformation Regularizations.
    Yuezhi Yang ,  Haitao Yang ,  George Kiyohiro Nakayama ,  Xiangru Huang ,  Leonidas Guibas ,  Qixing Huang
    SIGGRAPH (Journal) , 2025
    TLDR PDF Website

    We study the variation of shapes in the tangential latent space of a learned generative model, by enforcing piece-wise affine deformation regularization and analyzing the resulting tangent-space vector fields. This structure enables downstream 3D understanding tasks such as joint shape segmentation and cross-shape correspondence.

  5. GenVDM: Generating Vector Displacement Maps From a Single Image.
    Yuezhi Yang ,  Qimin Chen ,  Vladimir G. Kim ,  Siddhartha Chaudhuri ,  Qixing Huang ,  Zhiqin Chen
    Conference on Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition (CVPR) highlight , 2025
    TLDR PDF Video Code Website

    A generative model that produces reusable geometric detail stamps (Vector Displacement Maps) from a single image using multi-view diffusion model and neural deformation field.

  6. Discovering Design Concepts for CAD Sketches
    Yuezhi Yang ,  Hao Pan
    Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS) spotlight , 2022
    TLDR PDF Video Code Website

    We study the discovery of modular design concepts in CAD sketches, by representing sketches as constraint graphs in a declarative DSL and learning reusable concept libraries through self-supervised program library induction. The learned concepts capture reusable geometric primitives and constraint patterns, enabling structured sketch interpretation and more accurate sketch auto-completion.

  7. ToothInpaintor: Tooth Inpainting from Partial 3D Dental Model and 2D Panoramic Image
    Yuezhi Yang ,  Zhiming Cui ,  Changjian Li ,  Wenping Wang
    Arxiv , 2021
    TLDR PDF

    We study tooth inpainting from a partial 3D dental model and a 2D panoramic image, by learning a latent space of full tooth shapes using SDF representation and projecting the inputs into this space via neural optimization. An adversarial constraint on the latent code helps ensure globally plausible tooth geometry while reconstructing the missing roots.
